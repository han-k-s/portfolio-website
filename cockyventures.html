I was tasked with creating a wide variety of compelling social templates to market the services of COCKY Ventures. COCKY is a venture studio that invests in female-led brands with an accompanying podcast that breaks down the week’s biggest headlines in various professional topics, such as business, branding, and marketing.

Branding by Kelly Wiesehan

The What and Why

Using COCKY’s branding, I created over 100 templates in 6 different color-ways. I then formatted them for 3 different platforms (dimensions), generating a template library of over 300 graphics for a wide range of uses.

As the name would suggest, COCKY Ventures strives to be unapologetic, straight-to-the-point, and empowering.To convey a sense of boldness, I utilized dramatic scale shifts, and to convey movement and breaking of borders, I staggered and overlapped elements within compositions.
